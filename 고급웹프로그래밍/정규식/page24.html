\A는 문자열의 시작과 일치함. ^와 비슷하지만, ^는 여러 줄 문자열을 고려할 경우 각 새 줄 뒤에 위치함.
\Z는 문자열의 끝이나 newline 전에서만 일치함. $와 비슷하지만, $는 newline 하나 당 위치함.

case 1

Regular Expression: \A...

Ere iron was found or tree was hewn, When young was mountain under moon; Ere ring was made, or wrought was woe, It walked the forests long ago.


case 2

Regular Expression: ...\Z

Ere iron was found or tree was hewn, When young was mountain under moon; Ere ring was made, or wrought was woe, It walked the forests long ago.


=> case 1: 문자열의 시작 세 문자를 찾아라.
case 2: 문자열의 끝 세 문자를 찾아라. (근데 결과가 제대로 안 나옴.)
