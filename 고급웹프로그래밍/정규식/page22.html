\b는 word boundary(두 개의 캐릭터 사이의 경계)를 나타냄.
word boundary는 한쪽에 \w(alphanumeric)가 있고 다른 쪽에 \W(non-alphanumeric)가 있는 두 문자 사이의 점으로 정의됨.

case 1

Regular Expression: \b.

Ere iron was found or tree was hewn, When young was mountain under moon;Ere ring was made, or wrought was woe, It walked the forests long ago.


case 2

Regular Expression: .\b

Ere iron was found or tree was hewn, When young was mountain under moon;Ere ring was made, or wrought was woe, It walked the forests long ago.


=> case 1: 경계 뒤 바로 첫 문자를 찾기.
case 2: 경계 앞 바로 첫 문자를 찾기.
